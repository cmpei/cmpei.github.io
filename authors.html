Submission Guidelines

Authors should follow APA 7th Edition for both citation and references.

The full paper should not be more than 6000 words including Introduction, objectives, theoretical background and/or literature review, methodology, results and discussion, and conclusions. It is suggested that there should be a good mix of all the above sections within the word limit. Further, the authors are requested to ensure that the full paper conforms to the overall theme of the conference.

Names of authors, their affiliations, addresses (postal and e-mail), and phone numbers should be given in the first page of the full paper. Authors should submit their full-papers and queries to cmpei@nitk.edu.in

Publication

All submissions will be peer reviewed for acceptance for presentation in the conference. Select papers will be published in the edited volume bearing ISBN No. The conference committee is looking at the possibility to publish selected high-quality papers in Scopus Indexed Journals.
